how to grow apple tree

the apple tree has been around for millions of years. it is a amazing way to grow food and should be just as easy as the normal tree

ingredient's

  • apple seed
  • dirt
  • water
  • shovel

steps

  1. dig hole
  2. place seed
  3. cover with dirt
  4. water every day
  5. wait 6 years for first apple and for tree to grow big
