how to grow a tree

today i will teach you how to grow a tree. it is very simple with few steps. it can take a lot of time tho.

ingredient's

  • sapling
  • dirt
  • water
  • shovel

steps

  1. dig hole with shovel
  2. place sapling in hole with root down
  3. fill with dirt
  4. water every day
  5. wait 3 years for it to grow
